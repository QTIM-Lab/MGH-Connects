Mass General Brigham Community Connects

Purpose:

Mass General Brigham Community Connections helps connect colleagues who live in the same neighborhood during this extraordinary time. Whether you would like to find colleagues to carpool to work, go dog walking together or are looking for colleagues in your neighborhood to connect, please read on!

Overview

To get started, simply click the Login button below. You will automatically be logged in via single sign on.

Please click: "Post a New Request" for one of the following types of connections:

  1. Transportation (rides to work, carpooling)
  2. Dog Walking, Pet-Sitting
  3. Neighborhood connectoins
